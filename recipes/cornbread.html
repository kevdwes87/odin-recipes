Ingredients

  • 1 cup all purpose flour
  • 1 cup yellow cornmeal
  • 2/3 cup white sugar
  • 3 1/2 teaspoons baking powder
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 cup milk
  • 1/3 cup vegitable oil
  • 1 large egg

Steps

  1. Gather ingredients
  2. Preheat oven to 400 degrees
  3. Whisk ingredients
  4. Pour batter into pan
  5. Bake 20-25 minutes
  6. Slice and enjoy!
